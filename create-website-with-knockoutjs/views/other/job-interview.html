Appearing for Job Interview

 
I have seen people have fear about appearing for Job Interview and it is not limited to only job it's for everything
 
Here I just want to share my thoughts with you based on my experience and Knowledge.
 
You must have heard about a phrase 'To defeat the fear you have to face the fear'. But it's very easy to say that face the fear right? How to face them? How we can overcome from this?
 
 
So the very simple and basic solution to overcome from the fear is knowledge it is the power which can help you in facing the fear. Gain knowledge in your field, as you gain more knowledge in your field/technologies. you will see you are winning over the fear and gradually you will be noticing that there are no fears.
 
Knowledge is very basic thing in boosting up your confidence. If you have knowledge, you can easily talk and do a wonderful discussion on it.
 
Few points in brief for attending Interviews with full of confidence:
 
Resume matching your Skill sets:
Your resume should talk only about your technical skills you have actually worked on adding multiple technologies will help you in getting call but it will not guarantee you in clearing the Interviews also at some point in your heart you will go with fear. And you know, if you have fear then you are increasing the percentage of disqualifying the Interview.
 
Research about the employer:
Go through the employer website, it will help you understanding what kind of work company is doing and where you are going to fit there. Also it will give you few points for asking questions about organization.
 
Prepare for Common Interview Questions:
Prepare yourself with Common interviews Questions apart from technical questions.
Common question are:
  • Why You want change the Job?
  • Why you? Why not others?
  • If you have switched many companies, then be ready for answering why you so many Job switching?
  • What is your strength or Weakness?
  • What qualities do you have?
  • What makes you upset at work? How do you handle these situations?
  • Do you consider yourself successful?
  • Tell me a moment which you have done ( Related to your Job only dude :) ) and you feel proud of that?
There are so many question, I will try to come up with new article with all these questions and Answers soon.
 
Sharpen Your skills:
Sharpen your understanding on basics concepts and other skill set as per your Resume. I am from IT so I can give example of that e.g.
  • OOps Concepts.
  • Basic understanding of different data structures.
  • Practice for solving some small problem, because few companies ask problem for qualifying Interviews. You can practice for such problems from Codility or Hacker Rank There are N number of sites you just need to google it around.
 
Better Understanding of your Project/Assignments:
Before appearing for interviews, You must have better understanding about your recent Project/Assignments.
Following questions asked on Projects:
  • Functional side of your Project?
  • Architecture of your application, Explain the Architecture?
  • What Technology stacks? Why you are using certain technology in your Project.
  • What different Design patterns You have used in your Project? Why and How does it solving the Business problem etc.
 
Be on Time:
Be available on time before 10-15 min of the scheduled time. Don't be so early as well because it create pressure on Interviewer that You are waiting from long time and he/she might be busy in some on important assignments, this could be disturbing for him.
 
Well Dressed:
Prefer going in formal. Some companies follow casual as well, If you are going in casual make sure you are not in very casual :).
 
Confidant and Enthusiastic:
Be Confident and Answer question with Enthusiastically. Make sure you are not over enthusiasts :).
 
Answer specific to the Question:
Your answer must be specific to the question asked by the Interviewer, try answering with Example. But don't try to over exaggerate your answer. Make sure that your answer is not going very long.
 
Ask Questions:
If given chance for asking any question, make sure you are asking some questions.
You can ask Following Questions:
  • Is this discussion is specific to any Project?
  • What technology stacks you are using in your Project?
  • What will be my role in this Project? If Things went fine.
  • For first 30 days, what kind of expectations are from me, if I get chance working with this Organization/Project.
  • How generally you/organization evaluate new Joiner? etc.
 
Communication:
Work on your communication. Able to answer some tricky questions.
 
Keep Reading and Keep learning.
